Credits

True and Functional: Volume I, a project by Timothy Yanick Hunter, is supported by Oakville Galleries and the Toronto Biennial of Art on the occasion of their respective exhibitions, Timothy Yanick Hunter: Collapse and Incompletion at Oakville Galleries and Ngozi: We Might Listen For the Shimmerings, curated by Chiedza Pasipanodya as part of the Toronto Biennial of Art 2022.

Collapse and Incompletion at Oakville Galleries is on view from June 3 to September 2, 2023 [↗]

True and Functional: Volume I is commissioned by Oakville Galleries with the support of Partners in Art.
